## Welcome to PaperJet

Document processing has never been easier. PaperJet is a privacy-first document processing platform that creates custom AI workflows to process various document types and extract any desired information.

### What Makes PaperJet Different?

**Privacy-First Approach**: Your data stays where it belongs - with you. PaperJet can run fully locally, including AI models, ensuring your sensitive documents never leave your infrastructure.

**Universal Document Support**: From invoices and purchase orders to contracts and medical forms, PaperJet handles it all:
- Normal PDFs and scanned documents
- Images and technical drawings
- Complex layouts with mixed content
- Infographics and visual data

**Custom AI Workflows**: Create reusable workflows tailored to your specific document types. Our AI analyzes your documents and suggests relevant fields for extraction, which you can customize and refine.

### Getting Started

1. **Upload a Document**: Start by uploading any document to create a new workflow
2. **AI Analysis**: Our AI analyzes the document and suggests relevant fields
3. **Customize**: Review and customize the extraction fields to match your needs
4. **Save Workflow**: Your configuration becomes a reusable workflow
5. **Process More**: Use the workflow to process similar documents consistently

### Built for Everyone

Whether you're a small business processing invoices or a large enterprise handling complex contracts, PaperJet scales to meet your needs. With both cloud and on-premises deployment options, you can choose the solution that fits your security requirements.
