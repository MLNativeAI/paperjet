## The Privacy Challenge in Document Processing

In today's digital landscape, documents contain some of our most sensitive information - financial records, medical data, legal contracts, and personal details. When processing these documents through AI services, where does that data go?

### The Traditional Approach: Cloud-First

Most document processing solutions follow a cloud-first approach:

1. **Upload to External Servers**: Your documents are sent to third-party cloud services
2. **Processing in Unknown Locations**: Your data is processed on servers you don't control
3. **Retention Policies**: Your data may be stored longer than necessary
4. **Compliance Risks**: Meeting regulatory requirements becomes complex

### PaperJet's Privacy-First Philosophy

We believe your data should never leave your control. Here's how PaperJet ensures your privacy:

#### Local Processing
- **On-Premises Deployment**: Run PaperJet entirely within your infrastructure
- **Local AI Models**: Process documents using locally hosted AI models
- **No External Dependencies**: Complete functionality without internet connectivity

#### Data Control
- **Your Data, Your Rules**: Implement your own retention and deletion policies
- **Audit Trails**: Complete visibility into how your data is processed
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted at rest and in transit

#### Compliance Ready
- **GDPR Compliant**: Built with European privacy regulations in mind
- **HIPAA Ready**: Suitable for healthcare document processing
- **Industry Standards**: Meets SOC 2 and other security frameworks

### Use Cases for Privacy-First Processing

**Healthcare**: Process medical records and insurance claims without exposing patient data to external services.

**Legal**: Analyze contracts and legal documents while maintaining attorney-client privilege.

**Finance**: Process financial statements and audit documents with complete confidentiality.

**Government**: Handle sensitive government documents with appropriate security controls.
